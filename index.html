Grafton Times

1 June 1859

21-30 May 1859

We have been busy the last coupe of days getting ready to move. Willie cleaned up is office to get it ready for him to be gone for a week as Richard got land to open a blacksmith shop. We also make sure we are stock up on everything that we need(tools,seed,bullets and more). We end off on the 31st to be in Grafton by June 1st.

20 May 1859

Today we got to meet the Shrits and Barneys familes as well as Brother Tenney. We are still getting everything in order for us to leave in ten days. We also want to make sure everyone is well rested. Mary Bareny ask us if we know of a doctor. Willie laugh a little and said that he is the town doctor. She said that she as not been feeling well for the last couple of days. Willie took her into office and givin her symptoms Willie said that he belived that she was pargent. Mary went and told her husband Richard. They were both happy to hear the news.

1 May 1859

I am Nacny McFate,my husband Willie and I Live in Vigin,Ut with our four children. The tiwns, William and James then is Zion, he was born the day the first pioneers came to the Salt Lake Valley then come Leah. She is the last for now. Last year we were ask to go help settle Vigin were we live. Last week we go a letter asking us to go about a mile or so away to help settle a town which will be named Grafton,Ut with four other familes. We know the Platts and the Davies. Our children are happy that they are comming they got to know the Platts and Davies well. We are going to went until The Shirts and Barneys get to vigin to held out with Brother Tenney who is going to show us were we are going.

Willie is a doctor and as an office in vigin which he will keep open and go there daily becuase most of his patients live in Vigin. He hopes our old horse can do all that work everyday. Jack Davies and Frank Platt have been called to be inchange of planting the cotton and wheat with the help of the other brothen.Right now Willie is the only one that as a day job that he can not leave.

We were told that the other famiiles will be here at the end of the month with Brother Tenney. The other familes are bring down te wheat and cotton form Salt Lake. which will gives us enough time to get all our homes and land sold. We do not what to leave Vigin. We have loved it there but we will still see everyone we knew when we come to town to go to the store or church.
