11 June 1859

I am Nancy McFate. I am married to the most amazing man this side of the Mississippi William(Willie)Frederick McFate. We have three children on earth and two in haven, who we love dearly. Will and Fred are twins and are 5 years old. Then we have our sweet Evie who is was just born a month ago. We lost Sarah and Jane in Nauvoo when they contracted Malaria from mosquitoes. Many others also lost loved ones as well. We miss our sweet little girls. We left Nauvoo in with Brigam young in February of 1846.

We have loved our time here in Virign
