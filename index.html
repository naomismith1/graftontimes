The dairy of Nancy McFate
Saturday, June 11,1859

I am Nancy McFate. I am married to the most amazing man this side of the Mississippi William(Willie)Frederick McFate. Willie is a doctor and I am a nures we meet in school. It is rear if women get to go to medical school.

We have three children on earth and two in haven, who we love dearly. Will and Fred are twins and are 6 years old. Then we have our sweet Evie.. We lost Sarah and Jane in Nauvoo when they contracted Malaria from mosquitoes. Many others also lost loved ones as well. We miss our sweet little girls. We left Nauvoo in with Brigam young in February of 1846. After we got to Salt Lake we had our tiwn boys who we love. We even thought we could have children again. Then we have Evie our sweet little girl who is about a 6 month old.

We got a knock on our door late this eveing asking if we could meet with Brigam Yough our phophet after sunday meetings tomoorrw. Brigham Yough as been send peopel all over Utah territory. I wounder were he would wants us to go. Willie and I deicde years ago that we will go were ever we are ask to go. Willie hates to move because it means he will have to move his offcie again.

Sunday, June 12,1859

What a Sunday it as been..... We had our meeting with Prestend Yough and we been ask to go down south to setttle a town he called Grafton. He said that we will six months to get our things in order and everything we have here in the valley sold. Willie ask for a week before we give him our answer. On the buggy ride home, I ask Willie what was his thoughts on moving again. He said he wants to make sure that is where we need to be. He wants to pray about it when me and fast about it before making a choice.

I know he is scared to move again. Soon after moving to Naoov after we jioed the chuch we all got sick with malaria. Sarah and Jane were the sicks and they passed away. Willie had a hard time with it. There was nothing he could have done did. He did eveing he know as a docter to do. Then on the treak over to Salt Lake from Nauoo we lost many frireds along the way. I am most lost my dear William Frederick. He got trap under a wagon when helpiing push The Mcdonald's wagon out of the muck. Willie broke his arm and leg then he got supper ill with a fever and chills.

I know he is not saying it ,but it is hard form him to move is docotrs office and he as a lot of peopel who look up to him for the health. I also know that we will make the best chioce for us

Sunday, June 19,1859

After of parying, fasting, and talking about it we deicde that we need to go though with going were Yough as ask us to go. We even talked about it with the boys becuse it means they would have to leave their school and friends. I was little scared to meet with BRigam, what if we were makeing the worrng choice. I know that we did ,but I know that we are doing the right thing.

In our meeting with Brigam he aswned all of the qustion will had. He told us that the Lord is knwign how hugde this scarfieee is for us. He ask Willie if six months wil be enough time to get is office ready to move down. Willie said that he could be ready to leave as soon as the end of the week if need. Brigam was so happy to hear that. I on the other end was not. I think BEigam could tell I was neaves and said "Sister McFate, I told you six months at frist and I stand by my word".

Friday, June 25,1859

Today s the boys 6th briday!! How can they be that be already!! The boys want to have some frieds over after school today we of course said that they could. WIllie closed his office early so he could go fishing with the boys for their brithday. Willie cooked the fish that they caught In the eveing I starting to feel ill, I just hope it is nerves Willie made me go to bed He siad that he will clean the house and put the kids to bed.

Wesday , July 2,1859

What a couple days it as been. THe last I wrote in this book. Isaid that I feel ill. THe next day I woke up with a fever and coverd in bllod and I couldnt get out of bed. Willie took time to close his office to take carre of me. We are thinking I had a miscarrges , but we don't know for sure. . I never felt that sick in my life. Willie told me that he thought I was going to die a coupe of times. I am greftful that I am feeling human again.

Monday , July 4,1859

Today is Independence day! The town of Salt lake did some games and booths in town we tookd th kids too. It was also Evie's half bridhatday. SHe is just learing to crawl and is getting so much hair. I can't wiat until i can put pony tails in her hair.

Saturday , July 24,1859

Today we spet the day down at the river. We brough fishing poles and we fish for dinner. THe boys loved to fish with their dad. We all had a good time and enjoy the fish for dinner. On the way home Evie start to brun up. We hurry as fast as we could home. We spend most of the eveing trying to get her fever down. Willie said he is going to spend the night with her in his office so the boys don't get sick. He dosnet know what she as.

Thursday , July 28,1859

It as been couple of days and I am going to try to put my feels to words. On saturday Evie got a fever followed by the chills. Willie tooker her to his office so the boys didn't get sick. Late Sunday he came home saying that I needed to come with him to his office. Eveie was not getting better only wrose and he wanted me to spent whatever time she had left with her. I don't know if I could lose any more of my children. Hvely father already took Sarah and Jane he can't take Evelyn. After I saw her I know she was not going to leave mcuh longer. I prayed to heanly fater to give me straged to go though this again. I pray that Sarah and Jane with take care of Evie until I can get to Heven. It was hard to tell Will and Fred that their sisiter went to heven.

Today was Evie's funural. How do you have a funural for a six month old. Brother Glassmenson who is a stonemason donted a heandstone to us and Brother Sanderson made us a cakaset. They where both are bretfall. What a amazing turbute to our illte girl.

I ask Willie want he thought got evie sick so fast. He told me that he ever as seen anything like it. She had a fever and hives.. He notice that she had a bite mark. He thinks that she got sick by what ever bite her.

Monday , August 1 ,1859

We have four more months before we start our home in Grafton. We are trying to sell as much as we can. Our house we are going to sell closer to Decmember. We found homes cows, pigs and chickens. The boys where sad to see the anmails go. We were too. Willie told them when we frist get to where we are going we will get more.

Today we found out that our friends Henry and Hazel Shrits. We frist meet the Shrits back in Navoo. They came over with Nathan Tenney. Who is planning the spot for Grafton. Nathan brought with him a map to show us where we will be going. Grafton will be 307 miles away from Salt Lake. It will take us about a week.
