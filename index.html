Grafton Times

4 April, 1856

I am Nacny, I am married to the love of my life Willie. We were called to settle a town by the name of Grafton. It is about 1 mile away from Virgin,Ut. We live in Virgin and were are waiting for the rest of the familes to get to Vrigin before we headout to Grafton. Which will be by the end of the month. Willie as a print shop here in Vrigin and will come into Vrigin daily to work so he can still keep it open.
